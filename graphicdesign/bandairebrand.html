Bandai

One of the largest toy factories in the world, Bandai is a Japanese company that has been producing collectible models since 1950.

Most of the models produced are based on Japanese animations and also realistic models such as cars, airplanes and tanks.

Having had only three logos in the past, the current one has been in use since 1983. With the implementation of new technologies and markets, the idea of this project is to bring a facelift to the company's visual identity.
